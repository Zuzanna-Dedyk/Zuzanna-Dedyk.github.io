An invited seminar exploring how language, cognition, and multisensory perception shape flavour experiences across coffee, wine, and perfume. Together with Dr. Ilja Croijmans, we discussed the cognitive mechanisms underlying coffee sensory analysis and how scientific research can deepen our understanding of flavour communication, expertise, and sensory evaluation.
