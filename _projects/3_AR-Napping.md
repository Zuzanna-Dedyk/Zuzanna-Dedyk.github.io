The Augmented Reality Napping Table (ARNT) is a novel experimental platform that modernises the classic sensory "napping" method through computer vision and augmented reality. 

Using real-time object tracking and automated data capture, the system enables participants to create spatial maps of perceptual similarity while dramatically improving experimental precision, reproducibility, and scalability. 

The project validates ARNT against traditional sensory methods and explores its potential as a next-generation tool for studying cognitive representations across wine, coffee, and other complex sensory domains.
